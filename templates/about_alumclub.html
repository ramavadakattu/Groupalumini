About AlumClub

Alumclub is a solution for connecting and engaging alma matter of any institution in a simple yet convenient way. We created this platform to help you discover, communicate and know your alma matter so as to leverage your alumni capital for suitable benefits.

We constantly endeavor to make our solution meet the following criteria:
1. Simple: We focus on making things simpler. Simplicity is the fundamental and core philosophy of Alumclub
2. Convenient: We craft our product with features that bring in convenience at every step of alumni interaction right from discovery to communication to connection
